# Account & Data Deletion — PerunForce

**Last updated:** May 27, 2026  
**App:** PerunForce (`com.perunforce.perun_force`)  
**Contact:** abigor82@gmail.com

## Delete from inside the app

1. Open PerunForce.
2. Open Profile or Settings.
3. Open the data management section.
4. Select **Delete account & all data**.
5. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog.

The in-app deletion flow is intended to delete local app data and, if you are signed in, request deletion of synchronized cloud data associated with your account.

## If cloud deletion fails

If the app cannot confirm server-side deletion, it may offer local-only cleanup. In that case, local data on the current device is cleared, but cloud data may remain until remote deletion succeeds.

## Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app or the in-app deletion flow does not complete, email `abigor82@gmail.com` with the subject `PerunForce account deletion request`.

Include the Google account email address used to sign in to PerunForce. Do not include passwords or sensitive health details in the email.

## Data covered by deletion

- Account profile data stored by PerunForce
- Nutrition, supplements, workout, habit, and wellness entries
- Health Connect data copies stored by PerunForce, if any
- AI consent flags and AI-related app records stored by PerunForce
- Uploaded profile or progress media stored by PerunForce, if any

PerunForce cannot delete data held directly inside third-party services such as Google Health Connect, Google Account, or Open Food Facts. You can revoke Health Connect access from Android Health Connect settings.

## Retention

Deletion requests are processed as soon as reasonably possible. Some technical backup or recovery copies may remain for a limited period before deletion cycles complete.

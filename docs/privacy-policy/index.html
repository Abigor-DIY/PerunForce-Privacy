Privacy Policy — PerunForce

PerunForce is a wellness and fitness app for supplements, nutrition, workouts, habits, body progress, and optional Health Connect data.

Effective date: May 27, 2026
Last updated: May 27, 2026
App: PerunForce
Package: com.perunforce.perun_force
Contact: abigor82@gmail.com

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data PerunForce accesses, collects, stores, uses, and shares when you use the app.

2. Data We Collect

Account and authentication data

  • Google account email address
  • Google display name
  • Google profile photo URL
  • Internal user ID

Profile data

  • Name or nickname
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Height and weight
  • Diet type and goals
  • Profile photo

Health and wellness data

  • Health interview answers
  • Self-reported symptoms and medications
  • Health conditions selected by the user
  • Lifestyle and wellbeing entries
  • Health support entries and plans

Nutrition data

  • Meals and nutrition logs
  • Food names, brands, and barcodes
  • Calories and macronutrients
  • Optional meal photos submitted for AI analysis

Supplements data

  • Supplement stack data
  • Dosages, reminders, and schedules
  • Intake logs and supplement trial data

Fitness and body data

  • Workout history and workout sessions
  • Body weight history and measurements
  • Body progress entries
  • Optional progress photos

Health Connect data

After permission is granted, PerunForce may read:

  • Steps
  • Sleep sessions
  • Workouts / exercise
  • Total calories burned
  • Distance
  • Weight

After optional expanded access, PerunForce may also read:

  • Heart rate
  • Resting heart rate
  • Heart rate variability
  • Oxygen saturation
  • Body fat percentage

Health Connect data is used only for user-facing wellness and fitness features inside the app. It is not sold, used for advertising, or used for cross-app tracking.

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide core app functionality
  • To personalize wellness, nutrition, and supplement workflows
  • To sync data between devices
  • To display Health Connect data you authorize
  • To generate optional AI-powered informational outputs
  • To let you export, review, or delete your data

4. AI Features

PerunForce includes optional AI features. When you use those features, relevant data may be sent to Google Gemini for processing, including health interview summaries, supplement analysis, and meal photo analysis.

Health-related AI flows require explicit in-app consent before relevant health data is sent.

AI-generated outputs are informational only and are not medical diagnosis, treatment, or professional medical advice.

5. Health Connect

PerunForce uses Health Connect only after you explicitly grant access. The app requests a minimal scope first. Optional recovery and body-composition access is requested separately only if you choose to expand access.

6. Storage and Security

PerunForce stores user data locally on the device and may synchronize selected data using Supabase when you sign in. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS.

If you use the app without signing in, your app data remains local except when you explicitly use optional online features such as AI analysis, Open Food Facts lookup, or Health Connect access granted on your device.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase for authentication, storage, and synchronization
  • Google Sign-In for account authentication
  • Google Gemini for optional AI processing
  • Open Food Facts for food database lookups

8. Data Sharing

PerunForce does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers acting on our behalf when required to operate app functionality. PerunForce does not use personal or health data for advertising.

9. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as needed to provide app features, maintain synchronization, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements, unless you request deletion.

10. Deletion and User Choices

  • You can use parts of the app without signing in
  • You can disconnect Health Connect
  • You can decline AI features
  • You can request account and data deletion from inside the app

Public account and data deletion instructions are available at Account & Data Deletion.

If cloud deletion fails, the app may offer local-only cleanup and will clearly state that cloud data may still remain until remote deletion succeeds.

11. Children

PerunForce is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law or platform policy for the features being used.

12. Contact

For privacy questions, deletion requests, or data-related concerns, contact abigor82@gmail.com.
